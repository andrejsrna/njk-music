`Berts Jazz Bar` expands the [Jazz & Bass](/labels/jazz-and-bass) catalog with a tighter, club-lit EP built for listeners who want liquid drum & bass with more lounge character and melodic weight. If you are after **jazzy liquid DnB** with a slightly more intimate, late-night feel, `Berts Jazz Bar` gives you five concise cuts that move from groove-first opener to deeper, more reflective closer.
